These pages provides information regarding the managed computer resources within the School of Computing at Clemson University. These systems are located primarily within McAdams Hall.

Questions or issues regarding these systems should be directed to ithelp@clemson.edu.

Topics

  • UNIX Accounts - How to access your account, details about your account.
  • Using Webhandin - Instructions on how to use Webhandin - a custom web application that we have for turning in assignments to instructors.
  • Linux Workshop - A workshop we do for CP SC 101 and CP SC 210 students.
